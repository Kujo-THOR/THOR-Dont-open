Story about game

The player controls a creature resembling a snake, which moves across the game field (the field is bordered by walls), collecting apples while avoiding collisions with its own tail, the edges of the game field, and obstacles. Additional obstacles and bonuses are present on the game field. When the snake eats an apple, it grows longer, gradually making the game more difficult.
